Stop spending money on medicine you already have at home. Medz@Home provides intuitive, easy to use web, .mobi, iPhone and Android applications for keeping track of the medicine you have at home.

Your medicine records are kept safe online in a cloud database allowing seamless access whenever your need it. Know what's in your medicine cabinet, at home, and never spend a cent again on medicine that you've already got.

Medz@Home not only keeps a record of your medicine at home, but also keeps a health log on each member of your family. Know from your health log history what over the counter medicine worked for your sore throat last season or what symptoms to be on the look out for.

Be in charge of your family's health with Medz@Home. Not an apple a day, but Medz@Home will keep the doctor away.
